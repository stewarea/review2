Where should you travel next?






Alaska

Alaska is often refrered to as the "last frontier" in the United States. Here you can explore and hike to your heart's content. Alaska is known for picturesque landscapes, abundant wildlife and amazing seafood.

Guatemala

Guatemala is a jewel sitting in the middle of centreal america, waiting to be discovered. It has a rich and complex history, mixing spanish colonial culture with that of the native mayan culture. The mayan ruins, colorful textiles and amazing coffee are just a few reasons to visit Guatemala.

Vietnam

If you are a foodie that love to sample local dishes all day long, Vietnam is the place for you. The endless sea of street vendors cook up various versions of local cuisines, that are sure to please. Vietnam also offers dramatic coastlines in the north and relaxing beaches in the south. The mountainous regions to the west offer options to hike and sample the french inspired local dishes.
